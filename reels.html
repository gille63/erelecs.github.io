Firm Overview

Reelse Developers provides professional consulting services to investors, the design and construction industry. Our clients include corporate and bank owners, design professionals, building owners and tenants; and others involved in the construction and real estate development process.

Reelse Developers strives to provide value-added services to Owners, Facility Managers, Architects, Contractors, and other members of the project team. Areas of expertise include project development, owner representation, and project management.

Where practical, Reelse Developers brings creative ways of incorporating sustainability in an effort to optimize cost, decrease maintenance requirements, and maximize efficient use of resources.

We program our services to meet the unique needs and requirements of each client and project. Our assignments range from comprehensive management services throughout all project phases to specific tasks of short duration.
